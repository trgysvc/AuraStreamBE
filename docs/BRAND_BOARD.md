# Sonaraura Brand Board (Marka Panosu)

## 1. Brand Essence (Marka Özü)
"The Architecture of Sound, the Frequency of Intelligence."
Sonaraura is not a music service; it is an atmospheric intelligence ecosystem. It is precise, elite, and biological.

## 2. Visual Identity (Görsel Kimlik)
*   **Primary Vibe:** Deep Tech, Minimalist, Cinematic, High-End.
*   **Colors:**
    *   **Void Black (#000000):** Silence, the canvas.
    *   **Aura Indigo (#6366F1):** Intelligence, frequency, energy.
    *   **Starlight Silver (#E2E8F0):** Precision, engineering, clarity.
    *   **Biological Green (#10B981):** Life, health, frequency alignment (for 432Hz/528Hz topics).
*   **Typography:**
    *   **Headers:** Inter Black Italic (All Caps) - Strong, fast, aggressive but premium.
    *   **Body:** Inter Medium - Readable, clean, modern.

## 3. Visual Language (Görsel Dil)
*   **Textures:** Liquid soundwaves, glowing particles, deep shadows, frosted glass effects.
*   **Imagery:** Macro shots of audio tech, blurred luxury venue backgrounds, abstract representations of air and frequency.
*   **Logo Usage:** Small, precise watermarks in the corner or center-aligned for high-impact minimalist posts.
